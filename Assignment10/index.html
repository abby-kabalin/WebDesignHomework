When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?

5 Year Anniversary

Billie Eilish

March 29, 2019

When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? is the debut studio album by American singer Billie Eilish. It was released on March 29, 2019. The album was announced on January 29, 2019, with the release of the single "bury a friend". When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go? was an unbelievable success, winning multiple awards and nominations, including Album of the Year at the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards. Today, the album has 10.8 Billion Streams on Spotify, and is still a fan favorite. The artist has stated that her favorite song on the album is "ilomilo". The album was written and produced by Billie Eilish and her brother Finneas O'Connell.
